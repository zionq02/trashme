Mark Cuban

Born: July 31, 1958
Primarily Works for: Dallas Mavericks
University: Indiana University

Biography

Mark Cuban is a former principal owner and is currently a minority owner for the Dallas Mavericks in the NBA. He is also a co-owner of 2929 Entertainment. And is currently one of the main "sharks" on Shark Tank.

Notable Contribution

  • 1995 - Founded Broadcast.com in 1995 with Todd Wagner.
  • 1999 - Sold Broadcast.com to Yahoo for $5.7 billion ($10.6 billion as of 2025)
  • 2022 - Co-founder of Cost Plug Drugs to lower prescription drug prices

Famous Quote:

"Work like there is someone working 24 hours a day to take it all away from you"
