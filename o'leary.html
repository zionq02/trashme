Kevin O'Leary

Born: July 9, 1954
Primarily Works for: O'Leary Fine Wines and O'Leary Ventures
University: Waterloo and Western University

Biography

Kevin O'Leary is a Canadian entrepreneur, financier, and television personality who is best knowwn as a panelist on the reality series Dragons' Den and Shark Tank.

Notable Contribution

  • 1983 - Co-Founded SoftKey Software in 1983.
  • 1999 - Sold SoftKey Software to Mattel for $3.5 billion ($7 billion as of 2024)
  • 2006 - Appointed to the panel of venture capitlists on Dragons' Den.

Famous Quote:
